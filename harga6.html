Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags. Custom orders are final sale. Please contact our customer service team for return authorization.
How long does custom tailoring take? +
Custom orders typically take 4-6 weeks from consultation to delivery. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
Do you offer international shipping? +
Yes, we ship worldwide. Standard shipping is complimentary for orders over $500. Express shipping is available at checkout. Customs duties may apply depending on your location.
How do I join the VIP Loyalty Program? +
Our VIP program is invitation-only for clients who have spent $5,000+ with us. Members receive 15% off all purchases, early access to new collections, and exclusive event invitations.
